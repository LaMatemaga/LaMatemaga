## About me
🧮 **Mathematician turned Data Visualization Engineer** — I work in Marketing Analytics at **Zillow Group**, building dashboards, data pipelines, and visual systems.

✨ Outside of work, I run **[La Matemaga](https://lamatemaga.xyz)** — a Spanish-language science communication brand for LATAM audiences, where math meets code, data, and genuine curiosity. *"La magia está en comprender."*

📐 I'm a national gold medalist in mathematics olympiads (ONMAS 2008), co-author of a paper in Elsevier's *Journal of Computational and Applied Mathematics*, and I've taught 6,000+ students across online and in-person platforms.

🎤 I've given 40+ talks at local, national, and international events —from academic and technical audiences to the general public.
